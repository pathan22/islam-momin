    dua and dhikr

    Dua and dhikr are two forms of prayer and remembrance of Allah in Islam. Dua is a prayer of supplication, asking Allah for something or seeking His help. Dhikr is a prayer of glorification, praising Allah and repeating His names. Both dua and dhikr can be taken from the Quran or the hadiths, or can be personal expressions of faith. Dua and dhikr are recommended after performing the obligatory prayers, as a way of staying connected to Allah.
